###FONTS###

Cinzel:

Usage: Cinzel has a classic and elegant look, making it suitable for prominent headings or titles that need to convey a sense of sophistication and importance.
Examples:
Film Titles
Project Names
Section Headings
Playfair Display:

Usage: Playfair Display is a stylish serif font that can work well for subheadings and other places where you want to add a touch of sophistication without overpowering the design.
Examples:
Subheadings
Project Descriptions
Featured Content Titles
Montserrat:

Usage: Montserrat is a versatile sans-serif font that is clean and modern, making it suitable for body text and other areas where readability is crucial.
Examples:
Paragraphs of Text
Navigation Menus
Calls to Action
